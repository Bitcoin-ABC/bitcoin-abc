Features

Fast & Minimalistic

Lightning-fast transactions with a clean, minimalistic design. Focus on what matters most - your payments, without the clutter.

Secure

Your private keys are hardware encrypted on the device. No data collection or third-party sharing. Your financial information stays private.

Transaction History

Access your full wallet history in a breeze. View all your past transactions with detailed information at your fingertips.

QR Code Scanning

Scan payment QR codes directly from your phone camera app. Quick and convenient payments at your fingertips.

NFC Payments

Send and receive XEC instantly using Near Field Communication technology. Tap and pay with ease.

Watch Companion

Use your watch companion app to quickly request XEC payments on the go. Perfect for seamless transactions.
